Artificial Intelligence (AI) - The theory of a computer's ability to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making and translating between languages.
Watson - is an AI that was used to play jeopardy. It played actual games on air and won. It crushed its competitors. Watson can be used to help with data inquiry and faster analyze data faster for relevant information. But with everything, there are many ethical issues. Watson can be used to break in and access personal data​ and spy on people.
Google - is a search engine that finds relevant website depending on a search query. Google's code can help with data inquiry and  help with faster data analytics. The uses are very similar to Watson as their purpose is very similar. There are many ethical issues like information is now freely available and can fall in the wrong hands.​
Cleverbot - is an AI that talks back to a human. Cleverbot can help with elderly people who need assistance in daily tasks and can ease boredom with funny jokes and riddles.​ The technology can be later used in android robots that can offer public assistance to those in need. The ethical issues are big, as we are creating "life" and can be used in militaristic scenarios where we send in robots and not actual humans. The AI can also be used to replace humans in the workforce. 
​Cortana - is an AI that helps people with Windows carry out tasks that the user requests. The User presses a button and talks to the AI on what he or she wants to do and the AI does it. It makes automation of task easier and allows users to speak a task instead of actually doing it. The AI can be used for unethical purposes like trigger an explosion from long range. Many AI systems similar to Cortana, profiles the user and can be used to identify the user.
