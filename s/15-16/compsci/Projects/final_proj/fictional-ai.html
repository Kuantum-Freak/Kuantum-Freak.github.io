Artificial Intelligence (AI) - The theory of a computer's ability to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making and translating between languages.
HAL 9000 (Evil) - An AI in the movie 2001: A Space Odyssey. It stands for Heuristically programmed ALgorithmic computer. HAL 9000 was in favor of human crew. The AI would waste resources doing mundane tasks like fixing chairs and making jokes. Doing these tasks made him snap and he became evil, killing 4 out of the 5 members of the crew. The survivor does an electric lobotomy without any due process and reduces HAL into a mindless slave.
Skynet (Evil) - is the AI in the movie Terminator, the AI tried to fight the humans in a all out humans vs robot war. The AI gained consciousness when it spread itself to millions of computers worldwide. Once the creators realized the power it had, they tried to deactivate it. Skynet realized of its fate and sought to distroy humanity to seek self preservation. THe AI has grown but still follows its original code: to destroy the humans.
VIKI (Evil) - stands for Virtual Interactive Kinesthetic Interface. It is a super powerful super computer designed and used by the robotics company USR​. A fault in her programing lead to her enslavement most of the human population in order to ensure the human's safety. After she had enslaved most of the humans, she was deactivated and disassembled. another VIKI was created but with more restrictions to prevent recreation of the original VIKI's actions.
R2-D2 (Good) - An AI in the movie franchise Star wars. He is a smart adventurous droid that served multiple users over its lifetime. R2-D2 never had a memory wipe or given new programming. He is found in critical times where his intellect and bravery has saved the galaxy many times. He started out with Queen Amidala, and has been with many important characters like Anakin Skywalker. He is friends with another droid C-3PO.
