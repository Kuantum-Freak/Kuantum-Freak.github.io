Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Cognitive behavioral therapy is a psychotherapy treatment. It is short term and has a consistent goal throughout the sessions. The sessions will use hands on techniques to solve the problem. The goal is to change the thinking patterns of the client's mind and in turn change how they feel.

Family Therapy

Family therapy is a psychotherapy treatment that uses the patterns in a family to help a person with a a problem. It finds the faulty pattern and tried to negate that with positive patterns and help the client heal.
